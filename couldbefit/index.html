what it is

A gym log that respects your time — and your data.

Most fitness apps want an account, a subscription and your location. couldbefit wants none of that. Everything lives on your device in a local database, works with no internet, and stays out of your way: open it, log a set or a meal, close it. A single-purpose tool built the way I wanted one to feel — dark, fast and quiet — and shipped to Google Play.

features

Everything you log, nothing you don't.

100% offline

A local Room database — no account, no cloud sync, no analytics. Your training stays yours.

Meals & macros

Log meals and watch protein, carbs and fat fill up against your daily target at a glance.

Workout logging

Build routines, log sets, reps and weight, and let the app carry your progress forward.

In-app advisor

Guidance on targets and progression, built in — no forum, no guru, no upsell.

Dark & Compose

A pure Jetpack Compose UI, dark by default, tuned to be light on the eyes and on the battery.

Private by design

Nothing leaves the phone. No sign-up wall between you and your first logged set.
