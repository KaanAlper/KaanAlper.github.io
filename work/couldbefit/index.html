what it is

A gym log that respects your time — and your data.

Most fitness apps want an account, a subscription and your location. couldbefit wants none of that. Everything lives on your device in a local database, works with no internet, and stays out of your way. It even runs its AI advisor on-device — a local model you download once and then chat with completely offline. Open it, log a set or a meal, ask a question, close it.

the app

Log, eat, ask — all in the dark.

features

Everything you log, nothing you don't.

on-device

AI advisor, offline

A local language model runs entirely on your phone — ask about training or nutrition with no account and no data leaving the device.

Workout logging

Build routines, log sets, reps and weight, and see your last session inline so you always know what to beat.

Meals & macros

Log foods per meal and watch calories and protein fill up against a target set from your BMI and goals.

A local Room database — no account, no cloud sync, no analytics. Your training and your chats stay yours.

Calorie goals from your profile, plus optional ACSM-MET workout-burn added back to your daily target.

A pure Jetpack Compose UI, dark by default, tuned to be light on the eyes and on the battery.
